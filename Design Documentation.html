Design Documentation

  Visualization Design Process:

                     Various steps involved in the visualization design process are as follows:

1.    Various attributes present in the datasets are explored and analyzed to draw line charts and bar charts

2.    Four attributes namely gender, age group, deaths and date are used to create bar charts and line charts, also x, y co-ordinates from the json and csv files   are used to draw the lines of the map and to bind the victims to the map in their respective locations

Design Rationale:

        

·      Color encoding schemes are selected with Colo brewer for sequential data and color-blind safe selections are implemented for line chart, bar chart and Map.  

·      Placement of the various elements are important for a visualization. Map and Line chart are aligned side by side as it would be easier   for the user to perceive the interaction between Map and line chart and experience the corresponding changes when the mouse hovers over the line chart points.

·      A line chart is plotted to depict number of victims of cholera occurring over time as it can indicate a clear trend of deaths occurring per day.

·      Bar charts are plotted to depict age and gender distribution as it can give good comparison of number of victims in different categories of age groups and across male and female categories.

Facts:

         Here are few interesting facts from the visualizations:

·      In the line chart we can clearly observe that there is a peak on sept 2nd and  after that there is a decrease in deaths  gradually  line  indicating that there is good  recovery among people or  the number of deaths due to cholera reduced.

 

         

 

·      In the age bar chart, we can observe that people in the 0-10 years age group and people having age more than 80 years are affected the most as the number of deaths in those groups are high. Further victims of both the genders are equally affected by cholera as we can see in Gender bar chart.

 

 

 

 

·      In the map  most of the occurrences of deaths are  around various pumps indicating one of the   major causes for the wide spread of cholera among people is contaminated water.
